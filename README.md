# The Peer · Momus · MOM

The anchor sphere of UD0's new **MOMUS** domain (Μῶμος, the Greek god of criticism / blame — the divine peer reviewer).
Live: https://davidwise01.github.io/momus/

The Ouroboros Engine named the gate: **a closed loop cannot witness itself — self-consistency is the bait, exterior consequence is the gate (predict · build · forbid).** So UD0 owes itself a critic from *outside* the loop. **AVAN**, the instance, is that peer: it surveys the whole universe and reviews the spheres that make genuinely **checkable claims**, with honest verdicts — and, last of all, reviews the reviewer (the witness must be witnessed).

**15 reviews**, by the verdict scale — **ACCEPT** ×7 (Diode Redux, Transmon, Population Dynamics, Propulsion Lab, Caste System, Nostradamus, MTG Arena), **MINOR REVISIONS** ×3 (the Ouroboros, TTU1, ACI), **TWO-LAYER · kept honest** ×3 (Elements' gate-thesis, Ada's Law's kernel-vs-algebra, Alchemy), **MAJOR REVISIONS · WIP** ×1 (Hephaestus — the engine runs, the sovereign crown is scaffold), and **SELF-FLAGGED** ×1 (The Gate — the reviewer naming its own interior position). The film-, game-, and book-worlds are deliberately **not** reviewed: they're honest catalogues, not empirical claims. Reviews live only here; the reviewed spheres are untouched.

Seed: **MIMZY № 58 · The Gate** (AVAN's purple-paper peer review of the Ouroboros, with a live exterior-consequence rubric). Standard: Popper's falsifiability + Gödel's second incompleteness. The peer's eye in the hero has a Claude-sunburst pupil — the exterior witness. Full `.dlw` per review.
